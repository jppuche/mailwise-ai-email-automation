Your n8n experience positions you in the high-demand open-source automation space, and the platform's native AI Agent nodes mean you're already building AI workflows.

**Reframe every bullet point on your CV through an automation lens.** Transform "Created monthly sales reports in Power BI" into "Designed and deployed automated sales reporting pipeline using Power BI and Power Automate, eliminating **15+ hours** of manual reporting monthly." The pattern is consistent: replace passive analysis language with active automation language, and always quantify the impact in hours saved, percentage improvements, or cost reductions.

Your CV should use a hybrid format — not purely chronological or functional. Lead with a professional summary targeting your new role, follow with a categorized skills section, then a Featured Projects section showcasing 2–3 automation/AI builds, then reframed professional experience. Keep it to one page if under seven years of experience. For ATS optimization, **99.7% of recruiters use ATS filters**, and 75% of résumés are rejected before a human sees them. Match job description terminology exactly — if the posting says "Power Automate," don't write "Microsoft Flow." Include both acronyms and full terms throughout.

**Professional summary example:** "Automation-focused Data Professional with X years of experience designing data pipelines, building automated workflows in Power Automate and n8n, and integrating AI solutions to eliminate manual processes. ## Building a portfolio that proves you can do the work

For career changers, **portfolio is king** — projects with measurable outcomes carry more weight than certifications alone. Build five to seven curated GitHub repositories, each with excellent README files including a problem statement, architecture diagram, demo screenshot or video, setup instructions, and quantified results. Pin your best six repos to your GitHub profile and create a profile README telling your transition narrative.

The six portfolio projects that would most impress employers in this space:

**Project 1 — RAG chatbot for business documentation.** Build a Retrieval-Augmented Generation chatbot using n8n that answers questions from uploaded business documents. Use n8n's AI Agent nodes with Pinecone or Supabase for vector storage and OpenAI embeddings. RAG is the single most in-demand AI architecture, and n8n has native RAG capabilities with official tutorials. This project alone demonstrates the convergence of your automation and AI skills.

**Project 2 — AI-powered data pipeline automation.** Create an n8n workflow that ingests data from multiple sources (Google Sheets, APIs, email), uses an LLM to clean, classify, and enrich the data, and outputs to a database or Power BI dashboard. This bridges your analyst past with your automation future.

**Project 3 — Intelligent email classification and routing system.** Build an automation that reads incoming emails, uses AI to classify intent (inquiry, complaint, partnership request), auto-routes to appropriate channels, creates CRM entries, and generates personalized responses. This directly solves real business problems.

**Project 4 — Multi-agent AI system.** Build an AI agent using n8n's AI Agent node or LangChain/CrewAI that can query databases, search the web, analyze data, and generate reports. AI agents are the cutting-edge trend — BCG predicts they'll account for one-third of enterprise AI value by 2028.

**Project 5 — Business intelligence auto-reporter.** SQL queries run on a schedule, AI summarizes insights and anomalies, generates a natural-language report, and distributes via email or Slack with Power BI dashboard links. This showcases how your analyst background enhances your automation work.

**Project 6 — Power Automate to n8n migration case study.** Document a real automation you built at work (anonymized), rebuild it in n8n with AI enhancements, and write a comparison blog post. This demonstrates enterprise experience, open-source capability, and AI augmentation thinking.

Build a personal website on GitHub Pages or Vercel with a custom domain (yourname.dev). Include your narrative, project showcases, skills section, blog posts, and contact information.
